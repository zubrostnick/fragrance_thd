Ombré Leather Parfum by Tom Ford is a Leather fragrance for women and men. Ombré Leather Parfum was launched in 2021. The nose behind this fragrance is Sonia Constant. Top notes are Violet Leaf and Cedar; middle notes are Jasmine Sambac and Orris; base notes are Leather, Woody Notes and Tobacco.
